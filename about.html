About: EHS Resources, LLC

Protecting People. Preserving the Environment. Driving Compliance.

At EHS Resources, LLC, we specialize in delivering practical solutions that help organizations meet their environmental, health, and safety responsibilities with confidence.
Founded and operated in Florida, we provide consulting and training services tailored to your business and beyond.

What We Do
We focus on industry-specific assessments and environmental and safety training programs that empower businesses to protect their workforce, safeguard communities, and maintain compliance with evolving regulations.
From stormwater and air quality considerations to workplace hazard evaluations, we bring technical expertise and hands-on experience to every project.
What sets us apart?

Flexible, Specialized Expertise

Our flexible model combines in-house expertise with a network of specialized professionals engaged on a project-by-project basis. This ensures that every client receives the right knowledge and certifications for the job, without unnecessary overhead or generic "off-the-shelf" solutions.

Empowerment, Not Dependency

Even more importantly, we want to help you become self-sufficient. The programs, procedures, assessments, results, and material we create for you are all yours at the completion of the project. We believe in empowering our clients, not locking them into ongoing dependency.
Our Mission:
Protect people by identifying and reducing workplace risks.
Preserve the environment by guiding companies toward sustainable, compliant practices.
Provide value through clear, actionable guidance that strengthens operations rather than complicates them.
Whether you need a customized training program, a detailed sampling plan, or a comprehensive safety audit, EHS Resources, LLC is your partner in building safer, more resilient workplaces.
